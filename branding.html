Logo Construction Rules
Typeface: Playfair Display — "Quad" in regular, "Growth" in italic or bold depending on context
Colour split: "Quad" in Navy (#1B2A4A), "Growth" in Gold (#C8963E)
Minimum size: 120px wide on screen, 35mm in print
Never stretch, rotate, or apply drop shadows to the wordmark
Never use on busy backgrounds without a contrasting block behind it
Clear Space
1× cap-height 1× cap-height 1× 1×
QuadGrowth

Maintain clear space equal to the cap-height of the "Q" on all four sides. Nothing — text, images, or other logos — should enter this zone.

Voice & Tone

How QuadGrowth communicates — in emails, on calls, in proposals, and on social.

Brand Personality
🎯
Confident, not arrogant
We know what we do works. We don't need to oversell — results do the talking.
🏥
Specialist, not generalist
We speak healthcare. We don't talk marketing jargon — we talk patient numbers, chair time, and revenue.
🤝
Warm, not corporate
We're a small, sharp team. We write like humans, not agencies. Direct. Clear. Friendly.
📊
Evidence-first
Every claim we make is backed by a number. "More patients" is vague. "34 new patients in 60 days" is QuadGrowth.
